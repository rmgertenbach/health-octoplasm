Why Healthcare Transparency Matters

The same MRI can cost $400 at one facility and $3,000 at another. Healthcare transparency laws now require disclosure of these prices - we make them actionable.

Real Data

Actual negotiated rates from commercial payers and hospitals, not estimates. This is what providers really charge and what insurers really pay.

Actionable Insights

Not just data dumps - we turn transparency into tools. Compare providers, detect overpayment, build direct contracts.

Education First

Healthcare is complex. We explain how the system works, why prices vary, and how to navigate it effectively.
