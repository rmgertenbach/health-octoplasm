The Problem: Quality Doesn't Pay

You're a Grade A provider charging fair prices. But you're losing patients to Grade C hospitals charging 2x as much. The system is broken.

Bad Providers Win

Expensive, low-quality providers get more volume because TPAs put them in-network. You're penalized for being affordable and high-quality.

Middlemen Extract Value

TPAs negotiate "discounts" from inflated prices, then keep spread pricing fees. You get paid late, at reduced rates, with high denial rates.

No Differentiation

Patients and employers can't see your quality scores or fair pricing. You're just another name in a directory with no way to stand out.

Why Direct Contracting Works

You Win

  • Get paid fairly (Medicare + 140%, no lowballing)
  • Get paid faster (no TPA delays or denials)
  • Predictable volume (committed patient flow)
  • Build relationships (direct employer partnerships)
  • Quality matters (compete on value, not just price)

Employers Win

  • Save 30-50% (vs. TPA negotiated rates)
  • Better quality (you're Grade A, not Grade C)
  • Full transparency (no hidden fees or spread)
  • Better outcomes (healthier employees, lower costs)
  • Fiduciary compliance (meet CAA requirements)

Win-Win-Win

You get paid fairly. Employers save millions. Patients get high-quality care.

Everyone wins except the middlemen extracting value.
