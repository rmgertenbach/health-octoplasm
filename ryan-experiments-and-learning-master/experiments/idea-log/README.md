# Idea Log System

**Purpose:** Document the journey of building a healthcare transparency platform and establishing yourself as a thought leader in the space.

## Why This Exists

Building in public. Learning in public. Growing in public.

This idea log captures:
- **Product decisions** and why you made them
- **Strategic insights** from building and testing
- **Network growth** and key conversations
- **Personal development** and skills gained
- **Energy patterns** to maintain momentum
- **Ideas to explore** before they get lost

## File Naming Convention

```
YYYY-MM-DD_HH-MM-SS_topic-slug.md
```

**Examples:**
- `2025-11-13_20-30-00_initial-vision.md`
- `2025-11-20_14-15-00_first-employer-demo.md`
- `2025-12-01_09-00-00_november-review.md`

## Entry Types

### 1. Weekly Reflections (Every Sunday)
**Filename:** `YYYY-MM-DD_weekly-reflection.md`

Capture:
- Major developments this week
- Metrics movement
- What worked/didn't work
- Next week's priorities
- Energy level assessment

### 2. Breakthrough Moments
**Filename:** `YYYY-MM-DD_HH-MM-SS_breakthrough-name.md`

When something significant happens:
- Product insight
- User feedback that changes direction
- Partnership opportunity
- Media coverage
- Major milestone

### 3. Monthly Reviews (First Sunday of Month)
**Filename:** `YYYY-MM-01_monthly-review-[month].md`

Synthesize the month:
- Progress vs. goals
- Patterns identified
- Strategy adjustments
- Public sharing plan
- Next month's OKRs

### 4. Quarterly Deep Dives (Jan 1, Apr 1, Jul 1, Oct 1)
**Filename:** `YYYY-0[1|4|7|10]-01_quarterly-review-Q[1-4].md`

Big picture:
- 3-month progress
- Market positioning evolution
- Network impact
- Revenue/metrics
- Next quarter strategy
- Public article topics

## Examples of Good Logs

### Example 1: Product Decision

```markdown
# Why We Built Modals Instead of Inline Expansion
**Date:** 2025-11-13 20:00:00
**Status:** ‚úÖ Shipped
**Energy Level:** High

## The Spark
User testing showed inline card expansion disrupted the grid during presentations. Cards would jump around, making demos feel unprofessional.

## What Happened
Researched modern SaaS products (Notion, Linear, Airtable). All use modals for detail views. Implemented centered modal with dark backdrop, ESC key support, smooth animations.

## Strategic Insights
Presentation quality matters for enterprise sales. A polished, professional demo builds trust. Small UX details signal overall quality.

## Commitments
- Always test in "demo mode" before shipping
- Study enterprise SaaS patterns
- Prioritize presentation experience
```
