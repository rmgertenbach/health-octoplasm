## The Spark

We're building something that's never existed before - a transparency platform that doesn't just show data, but teaches people how healthcare actually works while giving them tools to take action.

## What We Built Today

### 1. Use Case Catalog System
- **What:** Complete interactive catalog of 33+ healthcare transparency use cases
- **Why It Matters:** Shows the full scope of what's possible with transparency data
- **Innovation:** Modal-based presentation perfect for demos and pitches
- **Impact:** Provides roadmap for next 2-3 years of product development

**Key Features:**
- Filterable by persona (Employers, Patients, Providers)
- Tiered by buildability (Tier 1: Now, Tier 2: Enhanced, Tier 3: Visionary)
- Data availability scoring (what can we build TODAY vs. what needs partnerships)
- Strategic value quantification

### 2. Modal Presentation System
- **Problem Solved:** The inline card expansion was causing layout issues during presentations
- **Solution:** Professional modal overlays with dark backdrop focus
- **Result:** Can click through use cases in demo without the page jumping around
- **Modern Pattern:** Follows Notion/Linear/Airtable best practices

### 3. Site-Wide Navigation
- **Integration:** Added Use Cases link to every major page
- **Visibility:** Footer links on homepage, nav on all persona portals
- **Discovery:** Easy to find from anywhere on the site

## Strategic Insights

### The Power of Use Cases
Having a comprehensive, filterable catalog of use cases does several things:

1. **Investor Pitch:** "Here are 50+ ways this data creates value"
2. **Product Roadmap:** Clear prioritization based on data availability
3. **Partnership Discussions:** "Here's what unlocks if we integrate quality data"
4. **Team Alignment:** Everyone knows what we're building toward
5. **Market Positioning:** Shows breadth that competitors don't have

### Data Availability as Strategy
The catalog reveals:
- **17 use cases buildable NOW** (Tier 1) - ship fast, prove value
- **16 use cases need enhancements** (Tier 2) - partnership opportunities
- **17 visionary use cases** (Tier 3) - North Star features

### The Gap Analysis
Critical data gaps identified:
- **Quality/Safety Data** (Leapfrog) - HIGHEST PRIORITY - blocks 8-12 use cases
- **NADAC Pharmacy Pricing** - Medium priority - affects PBM transparency
- **Outcomes Data** - Lower priority but differentiating

## Ideas to Explore

### 1. "Healthcare Transparency Report Card"
Annual report grading:
- States on transparency compliance
- Payers on data quality
- Hospitals on price publication
- **Impact:** Media coverage, policy influence

### 2. "Overpayment Leaderboard"
(Anonymous) ranking of:
- Industries paying the most vs. Medicare
- Geographic areas with worst spread pricing
- Procedures with highest price variation
- **Impact:** Creates urgency, drives adoption

### 3. "Direct Contract Marketplace"
Platform where:
- High-value providers list their prices
- Employers can "request quote" for specific procedures
- Automatic savings calculator
- **Impact:** Revenue model, network effects

### 4. "Transparency Advocate Toolkit"
Give employees tools to:
- Calculate their employer's overpayment
- Generate board presentation
- Draft contract language requests
- **Impact:** Grassroots movement, viral growth
