Why Price Transparency Matters

Stop Surprise Bills

Know your estimated out-of-pocket cost before you schedule care. No more $10,000 surprise bills after surgery.

Example: Sarah needed an MRI. She found one for $425 instead of $2,800. Saved $2,375 and got the same quality scan.

Quality Matters Too

Expensive doesn't mean better. We show you safety grades so you can find high-quality AND affordable care.

Fact: There's zero correlation between price and quality. Grade A facilities are often cheaper than Grade C facilities.

Cash Can Be Cheaper

Sometimes paying cash directly is cheaper than using insurance! We show you cash prices vs. insurance estimates.

Tip: If the cash price is lower than your deductible, pay cash and save money.
