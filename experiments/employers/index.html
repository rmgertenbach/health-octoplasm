CAA Fiduciary Duty: You're Legally Required to Act

The Consolidated Appropriations Act (2021) requires plan sponsors to act as fiduciaries. This means you must ensure healthcare spending is reasonable and necessary.

Your Legal Obligations:

  • Audit healthcare rates against market benchmarks
  • Ensure rates are commercially reasonable
  • Document due diligence efforts
  • Take action to remedy overpayments
  • Monitor TPA and PBM contracts for conflicts

Consequences of Non-Compliance: DOL audits, personal liability for fiduciaries, potential lawsuits from participants, and financial penalties.
